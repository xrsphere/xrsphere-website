TheraXR – VR Therapy

TheraXR is our revolutionary project for upper-limb rehabilitation of stroke patients. It uses immersive VR therapy, AI-driven patient tracking, and personalized therapy sessions to optimize patient recovery and progress monitoring.

  • VR-assisted upper-limb rehabilitation modules
  • Personalized therapy sessions adapting in real-time
  • Therapist dashboard to track patient progress
  • Pilot-ready program for hospitals and rehab centers
