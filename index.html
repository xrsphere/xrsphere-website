Flagship Project – TheraXR

TheraXR is our first revolutionary project, designed to assist stroke patients in upper-limb rehabilitation through immersive VR therapy. Combining evidence-based therapy with AI-driven patient tracking, TheraXR enables personalized care and measurable progress for therapists and patients.

  • VR-assisted upper-limb rehabilitation modules
  • Personalized therapy sessions adapting in real-time
  • Therapist dashboard to track patient progress
  • Pilot-ready program for hospitals and rehab centers
